Artifacts

Artifacts are powerful abilities that can be used but have cooldowns.
They can be found at the end of levels and every level has set artifacts
that they can drop, artifacts are often game changing.

The light feather is usefull for speedrunning and works well with verdant robe, it works as an extra dash.

The boots of swiftness is good for speedrunning, it gives a big movementspeed buff.

Death cap mushroom is the best artifact in general since it gives both movement and attackspeed.

The ghost cloak is godd since it gives movementspeed and allows you to run through enemies.

The gong of weakening is an artifact that weakens enemies making them take double damage for a duration.

The soul beacon deals damage in a straight line and it is very possibly the highest damaging ability in the game
