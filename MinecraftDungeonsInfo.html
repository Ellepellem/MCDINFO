Minecraft dungeons

Minecraft dungeons is a game based on Minecraft but changes it into more of a rouguelike.
In this game there are multiple levels that further the story which drop loot that you need to continue.
The loot can either be gear or artifacts where gear can be armour or weapons and artifacts are abilities.

Gear

Gear can either be armour, weapons or bows.
They all have pros and cons ranging from
range, damage, defence, speed and powerful perks.
Every piece of gear has their own unique giving them extra abilities.

More about gear

Artifacts

Artifacts are powerful abilities that can be used but have cooldowns.
They can be found at the end of levels and every level has set artifacts
that they can drop, artifacts are often game changing.
