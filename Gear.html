Gear

Gear can either be armour, weapons or bows.
They all have pros and cons ranging from range, damage, defence, speed and powerful perks.
Every piece of gear has their own unique giving them extra abilities.

Wither armour is one of the best aromur pieces because of the life steal so it can be used in most builds.

The verdant robe is the best armour for speed running because of the extra movespeed and artifact cooldown.

Fighters bindings is the best weapon since it hits fast and can easily proc on hit effects causing a ton of damage.

The diamond pickaxe is the best weapon for farming emeralds which is an important resource.

The harp crossbow is the best bow in the game, it shoots multiple arrows and works well with artifacts.

The auto crossbow is the second best bow because of its high fire rate and knockback.
